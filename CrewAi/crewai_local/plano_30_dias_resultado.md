# Plano de 30 Dias - Resultado

**Data:** 2025-10-31

## Executive Summary

**

To position the hotel as a competitive mid-premium resort in Paraty, implement the following strategy:  

---

### **1. Pricing & Occupancy Targets**  
- **Adjusted ADRs:**  
  - **High Season (Dec-Mar):** R$450 (aligns with market trends, avoids overpricing).  
  - **Medium Season (Apr-Nov):** R$290 (attracts mid-income travelers with value-focused packages).  
  - **Low Season (Jan-Mar):** R$240 (promotes long-stay discounts and bundled attractions).  
- **Occupancy Goals:**  
  - **High Season:** 75% (achieved via event-linked promotions, e.g., Festa Junina, Carnaval).  
  - **Medium/Low Season:** 45% (driven by family discounts, group deals, and free local attraction vouchers).  

---

### **2. Differentiation & Gap Solutions**  
- **Sustainability:**  
  - Obtain **LEED certification** (unique in Paraty) and promote eco-practices (solar energy, zero-waste policies).  
- **Cultural Integration:**  
  - Offer guided heritage tours, artisan workshops, and collaborations with local historians.  
- **Family-Friendly Services:**  
  - Launch educational programs (organic farm visits, craft workshops) and family-centric amenities.  
- **Local Gastronomy:**  
  - Partner with regional chefs for exclusive culinary experiences (e.g., traditional Paraty cuisine tasting menus).  

---

### **3. Transport & Accessibility**  
- **Inclusive Packages:**  
  - Collaborate with local transport providers to offer shuttle services to beaches, historical sites, and cultural landmarks.  
  - Integrate public transport options into booking systems for seamless guest experiences.  

---

### **4. Marketing & Sales**  
- **Digital Campaigns:**  
  - Leverage Instagram/Facebook to highlight sustainability, culture, and family-friendly features.  
- **Partnerships:**  
  - Collaborate with eco-tourism agencies and influencers to expand reach.  
  - Offer joint promotions with local businesses (e.g., restaurants, tour operators).  

---

### **5. Expected Outcomes**  
- **20% increase in reservations within 6 months** by addressing market gaps (sustainability, family services, local culture).  
- Establish the hotel as the **only LEED-certified resort in Paraty**, driving leadership in **sustainable, culturally immersive tourism**.  
- Achieve **75% high-season occupancy** and **45% medium/low-season occupancy** through targeted promotions and partnerships.  

--- 

**Result:** A premium, eco-conscious destination that combines cultural authenticity, family appeal, and local flavor, ensuring long-term competitiveness in Paraty’s tourism market.
